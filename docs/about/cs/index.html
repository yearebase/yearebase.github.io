Proč „Ye Are Base“?

Tato fráze pochází z básně Bastard Born od Voltairine de Cleyre:

Basely born! ’Tis ye are base!
Ye who would guerdon holy trust
With slavish law to a tyrant race,
To sow the earth with the seed of lust.
